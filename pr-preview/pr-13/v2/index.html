General

This dataset contains one year of CO2 and CO enhancement fields for 5 metropolitan regions in Germany (Rhine-Neckar, Rhine-Ruhr, Berlin, Nuremberg, Munich) at 1km resolution. These domains are embedded in the Germany domain at 5km resolution and the Europe domain at 15km resolution.

  • Contributors: Lukas Pilz, Sanam Vardag (GHG Simulation Group, Institute of Environmental Physics, Heidelberg University)
  • Time period: 1.1.2018 - 31.12.2018
    • reinitialization every 7 days from ERA5 meteorology (12 hours spin up)
  • Two different boundary layer schemes available: MYJ, YSU
